## Anti-Patterns to Avoid

❌ **Too Broad**: "AI" (returns millions of irrelevant results)
✅ **Better**: "AI news today" or "latest AI developments"

❌ **Keyword Stuffing**: "AI artificial intelligence machine learning news today latest"
✅ **Better**: "AI news today" OR "machine learning developments"

❌ **Outdated Modifiers**: "AI news 2023"
✅ **Better**: "AI news today" or "latest AI"

❌ **Ambiguous Terms**: "AI revolution" (marketing fluff)
✅ **Better**: "AI breakthrough" or "AI announcement"
