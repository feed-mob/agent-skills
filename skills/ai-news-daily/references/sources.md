## Quality Indicators

When evaluating sources:
- ✅ **Prefer**: Original research, official announcements, peer-reviewed content
- ✅ **Good**: Established tech journalism, cited sources, expert interviews
- ⚠️ **Caution**: Aggregators, uncited claims, sensationalist headlines
- ❌ **Avoid**: Clickbait, AI-generated content farms, uncredited sources

## Search Tips

### Site-Specific Searches
```
site:arxiv.org "large language models" 2025
site:openai.com announcement
site:techcrunch.com AI funding
```

### Excluding Content Farms
```
AI news -site:generic-content-farm.com
```

### Finding Original Sources
```
"originally reported by" OR "according to" AI announcement
```
